Inherits from NSObject

Overview

Payment operations are extra actions a user can perform in his wallet. For example, setting autoloading properties or transfering money to a friend. The MyOrder engine will automatically look for any subclass of MOPaymentOperation during startup. Note that the nature of the payment operations is very concrete to each individual case, so custom actions will be required on each case to complete them. Check the particular subclasses for more information
